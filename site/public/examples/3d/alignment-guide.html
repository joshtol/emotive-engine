Composition Tips:

Rule of Thirds: Place the mascot's "eye level" on the upper third line for natural viewing.

Golden Ratio: The sweet spot (0.618) creates natural visual harmony.

Hologram Zones: Keep mascot in upper 60%, emitter base in lower 15% for floating illusion.
